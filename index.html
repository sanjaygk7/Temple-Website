About Kudtheri Shree Mahamaya Temple

Kudtheri Sree Mahamaya Temple is located very close to Sri Venkataramana Temple in Car Street, Mangalore. The main presiding deity here is ‘Kudtheri Sree Mahamaya’ along with Shri Venkatramana, Shri Srinivas, Mahalaxmi, Mahalasanaryani, Shri Chandramaulishwara & Raktheswari Amma.

The temple was renovated and Puna Prathishta has been done by H. H. Shrimat Sudhindra Thirtha Swamiji of Kashi Math on 15th February’ 2000.
